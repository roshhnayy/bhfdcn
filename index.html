The Philippines holds incredible landscapes such as waterfalls, hills, and beaches.

Here are six (6) destinations you should definitely visit!



Papawyan Falls in the Philippines is a beautiful spot with cascading waterfalls and lush green surroundings, providing a peaceful escape into nature. It's a perfect place to relax and enjoy the serene beauty of the outdoors.

Papawyan Falls

Palawan, Philippines

The cove offers an excellent environment for swimming in its refreshing shallow blue waters. Visitors can engage in various activities like volleyball, cliff diving, snorkeling, and hiking. This remote island lacks the presence of Jeepneys, trikes, internet, or hotels, providing a tranquil escape from city life.

Nagsasa Cove

Zambales, Philippines

As the name suggests, Paradise Beach lives up to its reputation as the finest beach on Bantayan Island. With pristine, white powdery sand and crystal-clear waters, it's an ideal spot for snorkeling and swimming. The beach also offers opportunities for cliff jumping during high tide, and the tranquil mornings provide a serene setting for sunrise enthusiasts.

Paradise Beach

Cebu, Philippines

Beyond its spectacular rock formations, Mt. Pinatubo presents an adventure that is both thrilling and accessible. Despite being classified as a minor climb with a 2/9 difficulty level and a class 1 trail type, the Pinatubo crater hike promises an enjoyable experience for both seasoned climbers and beginners.
